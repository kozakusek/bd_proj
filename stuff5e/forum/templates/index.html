Welcome to stuff5e!

What is this?

This a simple website made for laboratories from Databases on FMIMUW. It is meant to show basic usage of connections to database (SQLite here) and even more basic webdevelepoment.


What can you do here?

An anonymous user can browse spells, races, feats and classes from a table top game Dungeons and Dragons 5e. After creating an account there raises a possibility for users to create their own custom versions of aforementioned.


Additional Features

One can also find a discussion forum here where users can talk on a whole variety of topics, a jurnals section - a place where players can write down stories from their games, and a character section - something everyone was waiting for. There one can create and manage ideas for their potential future in-game characters!
